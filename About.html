Catalyst

Catalyst is an online learning tool that helps students learn topics and skills they will need in the job market. Unlike other tools, Catalyst has a feature that can automatically update your resume depending on the classes you take and the activities or jobs you are involved in. It helps you build important skills that are useful for resumes.

List of features

  • A resume scanner
  • A skill development section to learn topics
  • Videos to prepare students for an interview
  • Programming tutorials for Python, Java, etc.
